Our Story

Timeless wisdom,
made for today.

PixieCare was born from a simple belief — that the purest skincare has been with us for centuries, in the gentle wisdom of Ayurveda.

Our Promise

“We redefine everyday skincare through the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda — botanical, sustainable, and made with love for your family and the planet.”

Where it began

A ritual passed down through generations.

Like so many Indian homes, ours kept its beauty secrets in the kitchen and the garden — kumkumadi for glow, herbal oils for hair, gentle ubtan for the little ones. Recipes trusted for generations, never a harsh chemical in sight.

PixieCare began as a promise to bring that wisdom back — thoughtfully made, honestly formulated, and safe enough for every member of the family.

What we stand for

Purity, potency & performance.

Every formula is 100% natural and free from sulfates, parabens, SLS and synthetic nasties. No shortcuts, no fillers — only botanicals that have earned their place over centuries of use.

We craft in small batches, test rigorously, and hold ourselves to ISO and GMP standards. Because skincare your family trusts should never ask you to compromise.

About Us

Founder Story

Crafted from a mother, for all the mothers

For as long as I can remember, I have always loved the idea of building a business with my whole heart. The part of creating something excited me beyond words — it really made me feel at home, like I was meant to do this.

Fast forward to being a mother of two, it all started with searching for the right product when I had my first child. I knew exactly what I wanted, and what was missing in the market — that's when the idea of building conscious, mindful products for kids was born. For four years, I worked relentlessly in the background, trying formulas for each product and brainstorming sustainable packaging. Staying true to our brand values every step of the way sometimes meant shifting the launch date multiple times, just to make sure every detail reflected our commitment to sustainability.

Ayurvedic, Chemical-Free Baby Care

Made for the softest skin

Babies' skin is soft and prone to a lot of skin issues due to environmental pollution. We wanted to formulate a chemical-free Ayurvedic product that fights dryness, rashes, itching, redness, psoriasis, eczema, atopic dermatitis, rosacea, blemishes and skin irritation. With the wisdom of traditional, authentic Ayurveda, we handpicked ingredients that help fight these issues in a baby's skin. Our products are made using Ayurvedic preparations and methods — without preservatives, artificial colours, sulphate, parabens, SLS, SLES, silicones, lead, petrochemicals or EDTA.

Our formulations nourish your little one's skin and eyes from within, gently cleansing while retaining essential moisture.

Sustainable Packaging

Kind to skin, kinder to the planet

Making every product plastic-free in its packaging was one of the biggest challenges — it consumed 70% of the time in this four-year journey. I almost gave up, until I heard a packaging supplier say, "You can't live without plastic." It made me sad how easily people accepted that, without seeing the bigger picture of what it does to Mother Earth. I wanted to break that chain. I wanted to do my part. Be the change I wanted to see.

We worked on every detail to remove plastic from our packaging — we didn't even laminate our product labels for looks, that's how specific we were.

Most of all, I wanted to build a brand rooted in true authenticity — one that is kind to Mother Earth, and a choice every mother would be proud to make for her little ones.

Here it is — crafted from a mother, for all the mothers ✨
